troubleshooting This article explains why a user or user group may have a different background colour in the Users and Groups section.

Why does a user or user group have a different background colour to the other users and groups in the Users and Groups section?

When you click the Delete icon for a user or user group, the background colour will change to red in the Users and Groups section. This means that the user or group will be removed when you click Save and they will no longer have this permission. If you do not want to delete the user or group, click the icon once again.
