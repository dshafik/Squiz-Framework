Why does a user group have a different background colour in the Belongs to Group section?

When you click the Delete icon for a user group in the Belongs to Groups section, the background colour of the group will change to red. This means that the selected user or group will be removed from the user group when you click Save. If you do not want to remove the selected user or group from the user group, click the Delete icon once again.
